## Featured Project: AirSmart Onboarding Platform

One of my most significant contributions involves the development of the **AirSmart Onboarding System**. This project required a high degree of precision in managing user states and data integrity.

* **System Architecture:** Built a modular backend using **NestJS**, ensuring the system remained maintainable as requirements evolved.
* **Real-time Integration:** Leveraged **Firebase** to provide instantaneous data updates, allowing for a seamless, "no-refresh" onboarding experience for users.
* **Problem Solving:** Resolved complex synchronization issues between frontend states and backend database records, significantly reducing user drop-off rates during the onboarding flow.
